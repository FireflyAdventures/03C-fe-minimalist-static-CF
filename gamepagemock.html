Key Design Ideas

  • Common Core State Standards (CCSS) will be built into a digital library of Adventures, the Teachers and Publishing company elucidate the real-world applicability of Math to students.

  • We use content developed by real Teachers and associated Artists. Our stories will be perfectly balanced between student interest and the required standards. Additionally, Teachers will benefit financially, we wish to mint more Millionaire Teachers in ALL of America.

  • Our software platform integrates with lesson plans producing a certificate of completion for each adventure and reduces teacher grading burden.

  • Engaging stories reward the most diligent of students with NFT badges of story characters and create alternative channels for teacher royalties.

  • Ambitious students will be able to work ahead of grade standards, should they wish, to fulfill a fascinating new Adventure.

  • Teachers will become Published Authors, a source of great professional pride and earn royalties through multiple digital distribution channels.

  • The Student Avatar can be modified to match the students self-perception lending to wider diversity, equity, and inclusion goals. Cost structure is deliberately kept low to enable broad adoption of AI tutoring, potentially worldwide with regionalization
